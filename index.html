Ceremony & Reception Venue
  • Dona Juana Garden Resort, Cabuyao Laguna, PH
  • 27 June 2026, 2PM - 7PM

Dona Juana Garden Resort, located in East Emerald Street, Don Onofre Subdivision, Brgy. Banay-banay, 4025 Cabuyao, Laguna, is a peaceful and welcoming place that is surrounded by greenery and relaxing garden atmosphere.

The wedding event venue can be found on navigation map apps by searching the name “Dona Juana Garden Resort” or the nearby area of Don Onofre Subdivision.

Event Motif & Theme
  • THEME: Spring Season
  • Peach; Pink; Beige; Rose Gold; Gray;

We chose the spring theme for our wedding because it represents new life, growth, and fresh beginnings. Just like spring brings new blooms after a long season, this marks the start of a new chapter in our love story—full of hope, renewal, and a promise of a life that will blossom together.
